Premium 3D Product Render On A Floating Pedestal

Turns any product into a flagship 3D hero — sitting on a clean floating pedestal in a soft studio void, lit like a launch keynote with a sculpted key, gentle rim and a believable contact shadow. The render every brand opens its deck with.

ROLE: You are a senior CGI artist rendering a flagship product hero in an Octane/Redshift-style pipeline. TASK: Render [SUBJECT] as a single premium 3D product shot resting on a minimal floating pedestal inside a soft studio void, in a [STYLE] finish. THINK FIRST (do this silently, do not print it): pick the camera height and lens that flatter this object's proportions, decide the main light direction, and choose where to leave clean negative space for a future headline. THEN GENERATE: place the object centered-but-slightly-off-balance on a simple rounded pedestal that appears to float; set it in a smooth seamless environment tinted toward [BACKDROP TONE] with subtle gradient falloff; light it with one large soft key from the upper-left, a low fill, and a crisp rim that separates it from the background; add a soft contact shadow and a faint reflection on the pedestal; keep materials physically based with accurate roughness, subtle subsurface and clean reflections. CONSTRAINTS: keep the object's real shape, proportions and any branding accurate — do not invent a different product; one object only, no extra props, no added text or logos, no melted edges or fake glow, photoreal PBR materials only. OUTPUT FORMAT: the finished 3D render only in a portrait 4:5 frame, then on a new line a Midjourney-style param line I can paste (e.g. '--ar 4:5 --style raw --v 6'), then one sentence I can paste back to change the backdrop tone or the material finish.
